About

My name is Dylan Obata. I’m currently an undergraduate student at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas studying mathematics and computer science. I’m also a researcher at the Real-Time Intelligent Systems lab, where I focus on applying computer vision to the field of action quality assessment (AQA). Currently, my interests lie in computer vision, machine learning, data science, and data engineering.

This site is a place for me to articulate my thoughts on various topics and to showcase my work. While I try to put out my best work unfortunately I am ignorant about my ignorance. If you see any errors in my work, or have suggestions on any improvements, feel free to let me know about it.
