Introduction

I’m currently about to finish my undergraduate studies in mathematics and computer science. And while my formal education is coming to an end, I find that I haven’t even scratched the surface of what is to be learned. What’s even worse is that I’ve found that the knowledge I have acquired disappears without constant use, and while I can usually relearn things quickly, I always wish that I didn’t have to.
